String Beans

Description

This garlic green bean recipe is a healthy side dish that is delicious and so easy!

Ingredients

  • Green Beans
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Fill a large saucepan with one inch of water and bring it to a boil.
  2. Add beans to the pan, cover it with a lid, and steam for four to five minutes.
  3. Heat the olive oil in a pan, add the garlic, and cook until golden.
  4. Add the beans to the pan, season with salt and pepper to taste, and toss well in the garlic and oil.
