Chow Mein

Description

This easy Fried Rice recipe is loaded with vegetables and only takes less than 15 minutes to make!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups cooked rice
  • 4 eggs, beaten with a pinch of salt
  • Prepped vegetables (carrots, onion, garlic, frozen peas)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • pinch of salt
  • 3 tbsp cooking oil
  • Garlic powder

Steps

  1. Heat a skillet over high heat and add 1 tbsp cooking oil
  2. Pour beaten eggs into skillet and scramble
  3. When the eggs are cooked 3/4 way, transfer the eggs to a bowl.
  4. Add 1 tbsp oil to the pan. Add the onion and carrots and stir for 3 to 5 minutes.
  5. Add the remaining vegetables and salt. Continue cooking for 3 to 5 more minutes.
  6. Transfer the veggies in the pan to the bowl with the cooked eggs. Add 1 tbsp oil to the pan along with the ginger and garlic, stirring for 30 seconds.
  7. Add the rice and mix it for 3 to 5 minutes.
  8. Add the cooked vegetables and eggs and stir to combine.
  9. Enjoy!
