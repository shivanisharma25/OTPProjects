Chow Mein

Description

This easy Chow Mein recipe includes noodles and vegetables with a simple chow mein sauce. It's healthy and quick to make from home.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Prepped vegetables (bok choy, bell pepper, cabbage, carrots, onion)
  • Garlic
  • Vegetable oil
  • Mixed sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles and place them in a strainer to get rid of all excess water.
  2. Next, heat the oil in a skillet over high heat and add in the onions, carrots, cabbage, and bell peppers.
  3. Stir fry the vegetables for about 3 minutes.
  4. After that, add in bok choy, ginger, and garlic.
  5. Stir fry the chow mein noodles and the sauce with the vegetables until everything is mixed well together.
  6. Enjoy!
